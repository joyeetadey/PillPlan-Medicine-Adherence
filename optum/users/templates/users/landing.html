Why PillPlan?

Imagine a world where there is no confusion on what medications to take and when, where providers can be given a complete picture of your health and well- being, where employees are connected and healthy. Sounds nice? We thought so too. That is why we created KnowYourMeds, a re-imagined digital health assistant which intelligently engages both the patients and providers for healthier outcomes in a highly personalized manner. Our platform acts as your buddy helping to monitor your health and guiding you in your journey towards a healthier, more engaged life.

Personalized Solutions for Medicine Non-Adherence

Automatic text and google calendar reminders to make sure you always take your medicines on time. Sync your care partners to let them know about your progress.
